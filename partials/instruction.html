Instructions to students :

  • At every stop there will be something for you to scan. either a flyer or an object explaining the purpose of itself for example, the course poster. or there will be a technology object for you to scan, for example, near the lobby. The Qr codes are on Some of the posters and objects.

  • When you have visited each of the locations, scan the code. you will be directed to an information page followed by a Quiz. You must take the quiz. The quiz is what you will all get credit for. The first group back to complete the quiz correctly with maximum points, is the winner.

  • Rules :

    1. Do not hurry. The faster you go, the less likely you are to win the extra 10 points.

    2. Do not split up: you all need to visit each of the locations.

    3. Do Not Cheat.

    4. For every wrong response be prepared to loose 5 points

  • Follow the clue which is provided at end of each quiz.
